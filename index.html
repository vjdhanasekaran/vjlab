Pathogen Evolution Lab
Pathogen Evolution Lab aims to advance understanding of the genetic and ecological factors that determine the emergence and distribution of infectious diseases. We conduct epidemiological studies and apply computational and experimental methods at the intersection of epidemiology, phylogenetics, population genetics and immunology to improve disease control strategies.
